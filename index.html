Why Love Sprinting with us?

Feel Healthy

Since it improves aerobic fitness, sprinting is a great way to help improve your cardiovascular health. Plus, it burns calories and can build strength. You can also escape your enemies much faster.

Feel Strong

Sprinting strengthens your heart by pumping blood more effectively through your body. This also helps with the management of adrenaline generated from escaping enemies.

Feel Free

Physical exercise, sprinting included, is known to release endorphins in your brain. These make you more energetic and happier. Your enemies will also have even lower self esteem than you, as they will not be able to catch you.

Get Social

When we sprint with others, we can go faster and farther more easily, and it can pull you out of a low mood. This is NOT to be confused with sprinting AWAY from others, ie your enemies.
